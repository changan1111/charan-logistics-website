    Privacy Policy

    We respect your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

    📅 Effective Date: 1 January 2026
    1

    Who We Are

    Charan Logistics Pte Ltd is a logistics and transport company based in Singapore.

    📍 101 Kitchener Road,#03-14 Jalan Besar Plaza,Singapore
    📞 +65 9185 8511

    2

    What Data We Collect

    When you submit a booking enquiry or review, we collect only what you voluntarily provide:

    • 👤 Your name
    • 📞 Contact number (booking form only)
    • 📍 Pickup & drop-off locations
    • 🚚 Service type, notes & review feedback

    We do not collect payment info, NRIC, or any sensitive personal data.

    3

    How We Use Your Data

    Your data is used solely to:

    • ✅ Respond to your delivery enquiry
    • ✅ Provide a quote for your route
    • ✅ Arrange and confirm your booking
    • ✅ Display approved reviews publicly on this site

    We do not sell your data or use it for marketing purposes.

    4

    How Your Data Is Transmitted

    Booking enquiries are sent directly via WhatsApp (Meta Platforms, Inc.) — no server storage involved.

    Reviews are stored securely in Database. Only your name, review text, and rating are stored. To request deletion, WhatsApp us at +65 9185 8511.

    5

    Third-Party Services

    Our website uses the following third-party services. These may process limited technical data such as your IP address or location:

    • 🔤 Google Fonts (Google LLC)Loads the website font. Your browser connects to Google's servers when the page loads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
    • 🗺️ OpenStreetMap & CARTOPowers the interactive map on the booking form. Map tile requests are processed by CARTO's servers. No personal booking data is shared.
    • 📍 Nominatim (OpenStreetMap)Looks up addresses when you type a pickup or drop-off location. Your entered address text is sent to OpenStreetMap's geocoding service.
    • 🚗 OSRM RoutingCalculates route and travel time between your locations. Your coordinates are sent to this routing service.
    • 🗄️ SupabaseStores approved customer reviews (name, rating, feedback, service used). Data is held on Database's secure cloud infrastructure. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
    6

    Data Storage & Reviews

    Booking form data is not stored on any server — it only reaches us if you press Send in WhatsApp.

    Review submissions are stored in Database and displayed publicly once approved.

    7

    Contact Us

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, feel free to reach out directly.
